States push again for a year-long daylight saving time

By Julia Ingram March 12, 2022

The annual switch to daylight saving time happens on March 12. For years, states have fought to end clock-switching, either by switching to year-round standard time or permanent daylight time, with little success. In 2022, here's where state bills stand, and what a yearlong daylight time might look like in the U.S.
